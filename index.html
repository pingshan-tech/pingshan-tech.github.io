關於

平山科技有限公司成立於2019年，主要從事碳納米材料技術的研發和產品的商業應用。

公司擁有多項在碳納米材料和鋰電材料的專利，並聯同國內外多家研發機構和廠家致力於相關技術的產業化工作。

公司亦活躍於資訊科技領域，開發了多種免費軟件和商用應用，並提供有限度的顧問服務。

Established in 2019, Pingshan Technology Co., Ltd. mainly work on the R&D of carbon nano material technology and related products.

We have a number of patents on carbon nanomaterials and lithium battery materials, and working on Industrialization and commercialization with leading institutions and manufacturers.

The company also active in IT field, released multiple freeware/commercial software products, and provide IT consultancy services by request.
